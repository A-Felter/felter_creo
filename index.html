Creo

Who is Creo?

Creo is an independent musician from Vienna, Austria. He specializes in making unique sounding and electrical music, lots of which include a saxophone. All of the art from his albums and singles are created by him. His most popular song is Dimension, released in 2016 in his "Virtualism" album, with millions of views on YouTube and played hundreds of thousands of times on Spotify. He isn't a very popular musician as his music is mostly only found on YouTube, Spotify, and his website. He has been making music since the 2010s, but only in the last few years has he gotten a bit popular.
